Roadmap

A phased approach to building, testing, and scaling the GreenChain Ecosystem from concept to real-world climate infrastructure.

From Vision to Deployment

Our roadmap is designed to move thoughtfully from research and design to pilots, mainnet deployment, and ecosystem expansion.

Milestones

Key stages in the development of the GreenChain Ecosystem.

Phase 1

Concept & Foundations

Whitepaper, tokenomics, and core architecture defined. Initial branding, website, and community channels launched.

Phase 2

Prototype & Pilot

Technical prototype of the registry and marketplace modules. Early pilot projects identified and tested with a small set of partners.

Phase 3

Mainnet & Integrations

Mainnet deployment of core infrastructure, integration with partner platforms, and onboarding of initial environmental asset issuers.

Phase 4

Ecosystem Growth

Expanded project onboarding, liquidity programs, multi-chain support, and enhanced analytics for enterprises and regulators.

Phase 5

Governance & Decentralization

Progressive decentralization of decision-making, community governance mechanisms, and long-term sustainability of the protocol.

Ongoing

Partnerships & R&D

Continuous research, new climate asset classes, and strategic partnerships to expand the impact and reach of GreenChain.
