Roadmap

A high-level view of how GreenChain Ecosystem evolves from concept to infrastructure for global environmental markets.

Phase 1 — Foundations

Concept, Research, & Community

  • Define core problem statement and solution scope.
  • Draft initial whitepaper and technical vision.
  • Launch social channels and early community spaces.

Phase 2 — Prototype & Website

Visibility & Early Experiments

  • Launch public website and ecosystem documentation.
  • Design prototype flows for asset tokenization.
  • Begin outreach to climate projects and partners.

Phase 3 — Testnet & Integrations

Network & Protocol Design

  • Launch testnet for core climate asset primitives.
  • Integrate data and verification partners.
  • Refine governance and token concepts with community.

Phase 4 — Mainnet & Ecosystem Growth

Scale & Adoption

  • Deploy mainnet for production-grade use cases.
  • Onboard anchor projects and institutional participants.
  • Support builders creating on-chain climate solutions.

Beyond

Community-Governed Evolution

The long-term direction of GreenChain Ecosystem is intended to be governed by its community and stakeholders, adapting as technology, regulation, and climate needs evolve.

This roadmap is directional and may adjust as we learn, partner, and co-create with the community.
